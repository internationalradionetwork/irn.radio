IRN Newsletter

Since May, 2025 the IRN hasa been publishing a monthly newsletter, which can be downloaded from this page!

Your input is needed!

We are looking for interesting content from our members. Do you have a story about a memorable radio contact, a member profile, a hardware review, a critter report or other newsworthy tidbit? Send it along, and it will be published in a future edition. Text and pictures welcome. Not a professional writer? Don't worry, we'll edit any submission to highlight your true genius! So don't be shy!

To contribute to the newsletter, please contact us.
