Frequently Asked Questions

This page is intended to provide you with detailed information about the IRN, including details on how to set up devices, how to access the system, nets, registration and more. If you need further information, please contact us, and we will reply promptly.

What is registration and why should I register?

We encourage all users to register with us. The benefits of registration are:

  • Full access to our network - If you are a licensed amateur radio operator, you will receive a password, granting full access to all connections, including rf gateways. For details on networks available to licensed and non-licensed operators, please see the connections page.
  • Custom callsign - if you are not a licensed amateur, you will receive a custom callsign to use on the IRN.
  • Confirmation email message - within 72 hours of your submission (usually much sooner), you will receive an email message containing your password (if applicable), your IRN callsign (if applicable), links to our current net schedule and connections, plus other tips to help you get started using the network.

You may register in one of two ways:

  • Option 1 (preferred method): please complete the form by clicking here.
  • Option 2: Send an email message to with "IRN Registration" in the subject line. The message must contain your full name, your callsign (if applicable) and your email address.

We look forward to hearing from you!
