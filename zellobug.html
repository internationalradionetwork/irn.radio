Frequently Asked Questions

This page is intended to provide you with detailed information about the IRN, including details on how to set up devices, how to access the system, nets, registration and more. If you need further information, please contact us, and we will reply promptly.

Why does Zello open unexpectedly?

The Zello Android app has a habit of keeping control of the PTT button in the background, even when its been told not to. This can cause problems if you use a second PTT app (like Teamspeak).

How to fix this issue

The problem is fixed by changing the mapping for the PTT button. The following will fix the issue.

  • Go into Options in Zello;
  • Choose "Push to talk buttons";
  • Remove everything except the "Screen button" and "Vox" button;
  • Click the + icon, and you'll be prompted to add a new PTT button;
  • Click the PTT button;
  • You'll be taken back to the configuration screen. Zello will have added two new buttons, one called "F12" and one called "Hardware Button";
  • Click on "Hardware button" and then choose the red "Remove button";
  • Click on F12, and uncheck (no tick) the box marked "Handle the button in the background when possible";

That should be it. Zello PTT will work when Zello is the active app, and you can use the PTT button in other apps when Zello is in the background.
